iCatcher Scoreboard Control puts complete control of your LED scoreboard in the palm of your hand. Designed for sports venues, community clubs, and event operators, the app lets you manage scores, timers, shot clocks, and advertising displays from your phone or tablet — wirelessly over your local Wi-Fi network. No cables. No complicated software. Just open the app and start scoring.

SPORTS SUPPORTED
• AFL — goals, points, totals & quarter display
• Basketball — scores, game timer & shot clock
• Hockey — scores, game timer & shot clock
• Soccer — scores & game timer
• Rugby — scores & game timer
• Cricket — runs, wickets, extras, overs & balls

SCORING
Tap + or − to instantly update scores. Every change sends the moment you tap — no delay, no extra steps. Scores save automatically and reset to zero after 12 hours of inactivity so you never start a new game with yesterday's numbers.

TIMER & SHOT CLOCK
Start, pause, stop, and reset the game timer with one tap. Set any custom duration. Choose count-up or count-down mode. Basketball and Hockey include a fully independent shot clock.

TEAM NAMES
Enter home and away team names on any scoring screen. Customise text size, colour, and alignment to match your board layout. Names save automatically.

ADVERTISEMENTS
Create up to 5 advertisement slots with custom text across up to 4 rows each. Set text size, colour, and alignment per row. Tap Play Ads to cycle them on the board. Tap Return to Scores to switch back instantly. All ads save permanently and survive app updates.

DISPLAY SETUP
Enter your scoreboard's pixel dimensions once in Settings. The app automatically formats all text to fit your board. Supports standard, ultra-wide, and single-colour displays.

DEMO MODE
No scoreboard? Enable Demo Mode to explore every feature safely — perfect for training operators before game day.

LAPTOP MODE
For venues running iCatcher Laptop software. Scores and timers are sent as counter and timer commands directly to the controller. Includes Page Mapping to control which display page loads for each sport and advertisement.

RELIABLE & PRIVATE
All settings, team names, and ads are stored permanently on your device. No accounts. No subscriptions. No internet required — everything runs over your local Wi-Fi. The app never collects or shares any personal data.

BASIC SETUP

Connect: Power on the scoreboard, join the same Wi-Fi on your device, tap Connect to Scoreboard. The button turns green when ready.

Start scoring: Tap Select Sport, choose your sport, tap Open Scoring, then use + and − to update scores.

Set team names: Tap the Home or Away field on any scoring screen and type the name.

Use the timer: Tap Set Time, enter a duration, choose Count Down or Count Up, then tap ▶ to start.

Create an ad: Tap + New Ad, choose row count, enter text, set size/colour/alignment, tap Save.

Play ads: Select the ads you want, tap Play Ads. Tap Return to Scores to go back to the game.
