── FULL DESCRIPTION ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

iCatcher Scoreboard Control puts complete control of your LED scoreboard in the
palm of your hand. Designed for sports venues, community clubs, and event
operators, the app lets you manage scores, timers, shot clocks, and advertising
displays from your phone or tablet — wirelessly over your local Wi-Fi network.
No cables. No complicated software. Just open the app and start scoring.

SPORTS SUPPORTED
• AFL — goals, points, totals & quarter display
• Basketball — scores, game timer & shot clock
• Hockey — scores, game timer & shot clock
• Soccer — scores & game timer
• Rugby — scores & game timer
• Cricket — runs, wickets, extras, overs & balls

SCORING
Tap + or − to instantly update scores on the board. All values send to your
scoreboard the moment you tap — no delay, no extra steps. Scores are saved
automatically and restored if you close and reopen the app.

TIMER & SHOT CLOCK
Start, pause, stop, and reset the game timer with one tap. Set a specific time
using the Set Time button. Choose count-up or count-down mode. Basketball and
Hockey include a dedicated shot clock that operates independently from the main
timer.

TEAM NAMES
Enter your home and away team names on any scoring screen. Customise text size,
colour, and alignment to match your scoreboard layout.

ADVERTISEMENTS
Create up to 5 advertisement slots with custom text across up to 4 rows each.
Choose text size, colour, and alignment per row. Select which ads to include,
then tap Play Ads to cycle them on the scoreboard. Tap Return to Scores to
instantly switch back to the live game. All ads are saved permanently on your
device and survive app updates.

DISPLAY SETUP
Enter your scoreboard's pixel dimensions once in Settings and the app
automatically formats all text to fit your board correctly. Supports standard,
ultra-wide, and single-colour displays.

DEMO MODE
No scoreboard? Use Demo Mode to explore every feature of the app with simulated
sending — safe to practice and train operators before game day.

LAPTOP MODE (ADVANCED)
For venues running iCatcher Laptop software as the primary display controller.
The app sends counter commands and hardware timer commands directly to the
controller, which iCatcher Laptop reads and renders on screen.

RELIABLE & PRIVATE
All your settings, team names, and advertisements are saved permanently on your
device. The app never collects, transmits, or shares any personal data. No
accounts, no subscriptions, no internet required — just your local Wi-Fi.


── BASIC SETUP (for help guides, FAQs, store listing Q&A) ──────────────────────

GETTING CONNECTED
1. Power on your scoreboard controller and connect it to your venue Wi-Fi.
2. Connect your phone or tablet to the same Wi-Fi network.
3. Open iCatcher Scoreboard Control.
4. Tap "Connect to Scoreboard" on the home screen.
5. When the button changes to a green "Connected" banner, you're ready to go.

Note: The default controller IP address is 192.168.1.252. If your network uses
a different address, enter it in Settings before connecting.

STARTING A GAME
1. Connect to the scoreboard (or enable Demo Mode for practice).
2. Tap "Select Sport" and choose your sport.
3. Tap "Open Scoring" to enter the scoring screen.
4. Use + and − to update scores — the board updates instantly.

SETTING TEAM NAMES
1. On any scoring screen, find the Team Names card.
2. Tap the Home or Away text field and type the team name.
3. The name sends to the scoreboard immediately and is saved automatically.

USING THE TIMER
1. On the scoring screen, find the Timer card.
2. Tap Set Time to enter your desired start time (e.g. 20:00 for 20 minutes).
3. Choose Count Down or Count Up mode.
4. Tap ▶ to start. Tap ⏸ to pause. Tap ⏹ to stop and reset.

CREATING AN ADVERTISEMENT
1. From the home screen, tap "Manage" and go to the ADVERTISEMENTS section.
2. Tap "+ New Ad" to create a new advertisement.
3. Choose the number of rows (1–4) and whether to show a border.
4. Enter your text for each row and set the size, colour, and alignment.
5. Tap Save. Your ad appears in the list and is saved permanently.

PLAYING ADVERTISEMENTS
1. On the scoring or manage screen, go to the ADVERTISEMENTS card.
2. Tap each ad you want to include (they highlight when selected).
3. Tap "Play Ads" — the scoreboard begins cycling through your selected ads.
4. Tap "Return to Scores" to switch the board back to the live sport layout.

CONFIGURING DISPLAY SIZE
1. Tap Settings (⚙) on the home screen.
2. Under PIXEL SIZE OF SCREEN, enter your scoreboard's width and height in pixels.
3. Tap Save. The app now formats all text to fit your board correctly.
