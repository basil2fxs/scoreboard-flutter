── FULL DESCRIPTION (3,329 / 4,000 chars) ──────────────────────────────────────

iCatcher Scoreboard Control puts complete control of your LED scoreboard in the palm of your hand. Designed for sports venues, community clubs, and event operators, the app lets you manage scores, timers, shot clocks, and advertising displays from your phone or tablet — wirelessly over your local Wi-Fi network. No cables. No complicated software. No laptop required. Just open the app, connect, and start scoring.

SPORTS SUPPORTED
• AFL — goals, points, full score totals & quarter display
• Basketball — home & away scores, game timer & shot clock
• Hockey — home & away scores, game timer & shot clock
• Soccer — home & away scores & game timer
• Rugby — home & away scores & game timer
• Cricket — runs, wickets, extras, overs & balls

LIVE SCORING
Tap + or − to instantly update scores on the board. Every change sends the moment you tap — no delay, no confirm button, no extra steps. Scores automatically save and restore if you close and reopen the app. After 12 hours of no use, scores automatically reset to zero so you never start a new game with stale numbers from the day before.

TIMER & SHOT CLOCK
Start, pause, stop, and reset the game timer with a single tap. Use Set Time to enter any custom duration. Choose count-up or count-down mode to suit your sport. Basketball and Hockey include a fully independent shot clock — start, pause, and reset it separately from the main game timer without interrupting play.

TEAM NAMES
Enter home and away team names directly on the scoring screen. Customise the text size, colour, and alignment to suit your scoreboard's layout and your club's branding. Names save automatically and reappear next time you open the app.

ADVERTISEMENTS
Create up to 5 advertisement slots, each with up to 4 rows of custom text. Set the text size, colour, and horizontal alignment per row. Add a border for emphasis. Select which ads to include in the rotation, then tap Play Ads — the board begins cycling through them automatically. Tap Return to Scores at any time to instantly switch back to the live game display. All ads save permanently to your device and are never lost after an app update.

DISPLAY SETUP
Enter your scoreboard's pixel width and height once in Settings. The app automatically formats all text and layout to fill your board correctly. Supports standard landscape, ultra-wide, and single-colour LED displays of any size.

DEMO MODE
No scoreboard on hand? Enable Demo Mode from the home screen. Every feature works exactly as normal — scores, timer, ads, team names — but no commands are sent. Perfect for training new operators, testing layouts, or demoing the app before game day.

LAPTOP MODE
For venues running iCatcher Laptop software as the display controller, Laptop Mode integrates the app directly with the software. Scores and timer are sent as counter and timer commands that iCatcher Laptop reads in real time and renders on screen. Includes advanced Page Mapping to control which display page loads for each sport and advertisement.

RELIABLE & PRIVATE
All settings, team names, and advertisements are stored permanently on your device — not in the cloud, not on a server. The app never collects, transmits, or shares any personal data. No accounts required. No subscriptions. No internet connection needed — everything runs over your local Wi-Fi network.


── BASIC SETUP (for help guides, FAQs, store listing Q&A) ──────────────────────

GETTING CONNECTED
1. Power on your scoreboard controller and connect it to your venue Wi-Fi.
2. Connect your phone or tablet to the same Wi-Fi network.
3. Open iCatcher Scoreboard Control.
4. Tap "Connect to Scoreboard" on the home screen.
5. When the button changes to a green "Connected" banner, you're ready to go.

Note: The default controller IP address is 192.168.1.252. If your network uses
a different address, enter it in Settings before connecting.

STARTING A GAME
1. Connect to the scoreboard (or enable Demo Mode for practice).
2. Tap "Select Sport" and choose your sport.
3. Tap "Open Scoring" to enter the scoring screen.
4. Use + and − to update scores — the board updates instantly.

SETTING TEAM NAMES
1. On any scoring screen, find the Team Names card.
2. Tap the Home or Away text field and type the team name.
3. The name sends to the scoreboard immediately and is saved automatically.

USING THE TIMER
1. On the scoring screen, find the Timer card.
2. Tap Set Time to enter your desired start time (e.g. 20:00 for 20 minutes).
3. Choose Count Down or Count Up mode.
4. Tap ▶ to start. Tap ⏸ to pause. Tap ⏹ to stop and reset.

CREATING AN ADVERTISEMENT
1. From the home screen, tap "Manage" and go to the ADVERTISEMENTS section.
2. Tap "+ New Ad" to create a new advertisement.
3. Choose the number of rows (1–4) and whether to show a border.
4. Enter your text for each row and set the size, colour, and alignment.
5. Tap Save. Your ad appears in the list and is saved permanently.

PLAYING ADVERTISEMENTS
1. On the scoring or manage screen, go to the ADVERTISEMENTS card.
2. Tap each ad you want to include (they highlight when selected).
3. Tap "Play Ads" — the scoreboard begins cycling through your selected ads.
4. Tap "Return to Scores" to switch the board back to the live sport layout.

CONFIGURING DISPLAY SIZE
1. Tap Settings (⚙) on the home screen.
2. Under PIXEL SIZE OF SCREEN, enter your scoreboard's width and height in pixels.
3. Tap Save. The app now formats all text to fit your board correctly.
