Privacy Policy

Scoreboard Control — Effective Date: 18 March 2026

Short version: Scoreboard Control does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. Ever. The app works entirely on your local network and your device.

1. Who We Are

Scoreboard Control is developed and owned by Basil Toufexis (“Developer”, “we”, “us”). This Privacy Policy explains how the Scoreboard Control mobile application handles information.

Contact: basil@icatcher.com.au

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Scoreboard Control does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, advertising IDs, or similar)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or crash reporting sent to any server
  • Browsing history or activity outside the app
  • Camera, microphone, or contacts data
  • Payment or financial information

3. What the App Does on Your Device

Scoreboard Control stores a small amount of configuration data locally on your device only. This includes:

  • Selected sport type (e.g. Basketball, Hockey, AFL)
  • Display settings (scoreboard IP address, port, screen size)
  • Advertising text you create within the app
  • Timer and shot-clock default values
  • Team names and colour preferences

This data never leaves your device except as scoreboard control commands sent over your local Wi-Fi network to the scoreboard hardware you choose to connect to. It is not transmitted to the Developer, any third party, or any cloud service.

4. Local Network Communication

The app sends UDP packets to a scoreboard controller on your local network using an IP address and port you configure. These packets contain scoreboard display commands only (scores, timers, team names, advertising text). No personal information is included in these packets, and they never leave your local network.

The app requests Local Network permission on iOS solely to enable this communication.

5. Third-Party Services

Scoreboard Control does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or tracking services. There are no SDKs from Facebook, Google Analytics, Firebase, Crashlytics, Sentry, or similar services embedded in the app.

6. Children’s Privacy

Scoreboard Control is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children. Because the app collects no information from any user, there is no additional risk to children.

7. Data Security

Because Scoreboard Control does not collect, transmit, or store personal information on any server, there is no personal data to secure beyond the settings stored locally on your device, which are protected by your device’s own security measures.

8. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with a revised effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
